### Steps (6 — maps to the existing MESH_STEPS)

**Step 01 — We ask your customer's question across five AI engines**
Not keyword tools — real buyer queries sampled live from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. We capture who gets cited, and for what.

**Step 02 — We map the gap: you vs. your competitor**

Missing schema. Thin content. Broken crawl paths. WCAG violations. All surfaced together, across real rendered page states — not a static snapshot. What breaks a screen reader often breaks an AI crawler too.

**Step 04 — Every gap becomes a ranked, scoped fix**
Impact vs. effort. Paste-ready. Prioritized so you know what to do first. No 200-item checklist that collects dust.

**Step 05 — A human reviews every change before it ships**
The system finds and ranks. A human goblin approves. Nothing auto-deploys — not schema, not content, not a comma. Agentic, but accountable.

**Step 06 — It re-runs on a schedule. You see the delta.**
The pipeline loops on a cadence. Each run produces a before/after scorecard. Visibility, citations, SEO health, and accessibility coverage become tracked numbers — not a one-time vibe check.

### Closing line (bottom of section)
The technical name for the system is a self-healing RAG pipeline on a CI/CD eval gate. The plain-language version: an automated system finds the gaps, proves fixes work, a human ships them, and you watch the numbers move.
